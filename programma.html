Program & Events

Discover the full schedule of the Camp

Lecture

Every day 4 Lectures of 45 minutes from Top Teachers.
(Friday Only 2 Lectures).

Practice Session

Every day 2 hours of practice session included warming up and stretching before and after the session.

Last day: Match Analysis with Score.

Group Lessons

Every day 6 Group Lessons (Standard/Latin) from all the Teachers invited to the Camp.

(Friday only 3 lessons).

Gymnastic

Every day 1 hour and half of preparation made by a top Gymnastic teacher to teach the correct way of physical preparation.

Middle Practice

Every day during Lunch time: practice session of one hour with a teacher to control and manage the session.

Private Lessons

Every day you have a chance to book private lessons with the teachers invited to Tuscany Camp.
